Classic Cut-Out Sugar Cookies

The quintessential Christmas cookie. Perfect for decorating with royal icing and sprinkles.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Powdered sugar (for rolling out)

Instructions

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: Sift together flour and baking powder in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Cream Butter & Sugar: In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg and milk; beat to combine.
  3. Combine: Gradually add flour mixture to butter mixture; beat until just combined.
  4. Chill: Divide dough in half, wrap in waxed paper, and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  5. Preheat & Roll: Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C). Sprinkle surface with powdered sugar and roll out dough to 1/4-inch thickness.
  6. Cut & Bake: Cut into festive shapes using cookie cutters. Place on greased baking sheets. Bake for 7-9 minutes or until edges are firm.
  7. Cool: Cool on wire racks completely before decorating.

Spiced Gingerbread Men

Warm spices and molasses make this sturdy cookie a holiday favorite for building houses or little men.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2/3 cup molasses
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Whisk Spices: Whisk together flour, baking soda, ginger, cloves, and cinnamon in a medium bowl.
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a separate large bowl, beat butter, brown sugar, and egg until fluffy. Stir in molasses.
  3. Form Dough: Gradually stir the flour mixture into the molasses mixture until a stiff dough forms.
  4. Chill: Divide dough, wrap in plastic, and chill for at least 1 hour.
  5. Preheat: Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  6. Roll & Cut: Roll dough out to 1/4 inch thickness. Cut with gingerbread man cutters.
  7. Bake: Place cookies 1 inch apart on baking sheets. Bake for 8-10 minutes. Cool on wire racks.

Pecan Snowballs (Russian Tea Cakes)

Buttery, nutty, and coated in "snow," these melt-in-your-mouth cookies are incredibly easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup finely chopped pecans
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Extra powdered sugar for coating

Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat oven to 400¬∞F (200¬∞C).
  2. Mix Butter & Sugar: Mix butter, 1/2 cup powdered sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.
  3. Add Flour & Nuts: Stir in flour, salt, and nuts until dough holds together.
  4. Shape: Shape dough into 1-inch balls. Place on ungreased baking sheets.
  5. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes until set but not brown.
  6. Coat: While still warm, roll cookies in extra powdered sugar. Let cool, then roll in sugar again for a snowy look.
